Inflation Rate of Countries in year 2021

The graph below shows the inflation rate of countries in the year 2021. Next, there are 179 countries in the map chart below. However, there are only four countries that have text annotations. For example, Australia, Brazil, Canada and China. Besides that, the inflation rate of each country is differentiated by colour saturation. The lower the colour saturation, the lower the value of the inflation rate of the following country.

CPI Annual Movement of Goods and Services in Australia

The graph below shows the Consumer Price Index (CPI) Annual Movement of Goods and Services in Australia. Next, the values of the CPI Annual Movement of Goods and Services are shown from the year 2003 to the year 2021. Based on the stacked bar chart below, it proves that the value of CPI Annual Movement of Goods and Services in the year 2008 is the highest which is 4.4 in total. Conversely, the value of CPI Annual Movement of Goods and Services in the year 2020 is the lowest which is 0.8 in total. Lastly, either the values of the CPI Annual Movement of Goods or the values of the CPI Annual Movement of Services can be shown by selecting the Category legend.

CPI Percentage Change (%) for year 2022 in Australia

The graph below shows the Consumer Price Index (CPI) Percentage Change (%) for the year 2022 in Australia. Next, four months are shown in the line graph below which are March, June, September and December. Based on the line graph below, it shows that the value of the Consumer Price Index (CPI) Percentage Change (%) on 8th September 2022 is the highest which is 5%. Conversely, the value of the Consumer Price Index (CPI) Percentage Change (%) on 20th June 2022 is the lowest which is -0.3%. Lastly, the line graph of a specific month can be shown by selecting the Month legend or by filtering the drop-down menu.
